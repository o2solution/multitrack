About Us

We are one of the leading GPS Tracking platforms across the globe with over 1 lakh vehicles onboarded in 5 continents and over 35 countries. Our platform provides user friendly interfaces with wide ranging tracking parameters such as speed, location, idling and stoppage of the vehicle, device alerts, distance and other types of data parameters. Platform provides numerous reports to keep track on the vehicle trips and various App notifications covering critical aspects of the vehicle tracking. The platform is integrated with the cloud infrastructure to enable users access anytime anywhere and almost 99.99% uptime. The competitive pricing and robust features with end to end integrations makes it a very attractive option for large fleet owners. We also provide customization to suit customer need and various industry practices.
